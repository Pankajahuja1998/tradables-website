The Story

The Journey of Tradables

It started with a harsh lesson in the markets during college. I was a Mechanical Engineering student who realized that the real engineering problem wasn't machines—it was risk. That early setback sparked a relentless obsession with the markets. I threw out the noise, studied the data, and built a systematic, rule-based approach to trading.

At Tradables, we believe the market is supreme. Our proprietary trading desk is built on one simple rule: protect the capital first, let the returns follow. By combining strict risk-management with non-directional option selling, we capture steady growth while fiercely guarding the downside.

But the journey didn't stop at the markets. My experience leading B2B SaaS sales and successfully co-founding a startup gave me a unique blueprint for business scaling. Today, Tradables leverages that battle-tested knowledge to help visionary startups secure funding, craft winning pitch decks, and connect with the right investors.
